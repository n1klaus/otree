Instructions

  1. You are in a group of {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} people. Player 1 is endowed with KSH 200 and will be asked to choose any amount between KSH 0 and KSH 200 to send to Player 2.
  2. Player 3 sees the amount that Player 1 has decided to send to Player 2 and from this, he will decide to either Punish or Not Punish Player 1.
  3. If Player 3 decides to punish the offer, both Player 1 and Player 2 will not receive any payout.
  4. If Player 3 decides not to punish the offer, the payout for Player 1 will be KSH 200 - the offer amount and Player 2 will receive the amount that Player 1 decided to send him.
  5. Player 1 and Player 2 will be shown their payouts in the results page.

This game will be played for {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds.
